Meet the Founder

Esther Yee has been creating headbands since 2005. Her goal was to make headbands the best quality, while maintaining an affordable price. In 2019, Esther thought creating a website would be the best way to present her products.

Isaac Junus created Esvida's website design in late April of 2020. Esther asked her son personally to create the website from scratch using programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
